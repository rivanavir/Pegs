Welcome to pegs unlimited

Pegs Unlimited is New Zealand's leading Boxing Peg and Garden Stake Manufacturer. Over the last 8 years we have made millions of Pegs and Stakes, in almost any conceivable size and length. With over 120 different sizes and lengths in stock, if that doesn't get what you want we offer a full made to order service.

Whether it is Pine Pegs, Douglas Fir (Oregon) Pegs, Hardwood, Untreated, H4 Treated we make them. Any length, any size. We send product all over New Zealand, and along with our Nationwide network of stockists, you have access to the best product. If you want Boxing Pegs and Garden stakes we supply them - contact us for your nearest stockist.
